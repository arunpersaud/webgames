How to play Skat:

  • Find 3 players, start a video conference. Every player will need a deck of cards
  • All players enter the same text in the field below
  • The next page will show 4 links. These pages are only shown once. Agree which player clicks on which links
  • Pick the cards you are shown out of the deck, put the remaining cards away
  • Start playing by showing the card you are playing in the video stream
  • Every player makes three piles and puts his card on the pile of the person who won the trick
  • At the end, count the points in each pile, compare and add up. repeat
